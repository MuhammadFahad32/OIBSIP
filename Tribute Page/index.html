About Dr. Abdul Qadeer Khan

Dr. Abdul Qadeer Khan (1936–2021) was one of Pakistan's most respected scientists, metallurgical engineers, and national heroes. He played a vital role in developing Pakistan's nuclear technology and is widely known as the father of Pakistan's nuclear program. Through his dedication, scientific expertise, and visionary leadership, he strengthened Pakistan's defense capabilities and inspired generations of young scientists to pursue excellence in science, technology, and innovation.
